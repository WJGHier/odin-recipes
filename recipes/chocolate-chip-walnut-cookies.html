Homemade Chocolate Chip and Walnut Cookies

Delicious Homemade Chocolate Chip and Walnut Cookies

If you are looking for the best cookies you've ever tasted, you're in luck. Kata's Chocolate Chip and Walnut Cookie recipe is the best cookie recipe you will ever come across, web or otherwise.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cold and cubed unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3.4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 1/4 cup of flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (1 milk chocalte and 1 semi-sweet chocolate)
  • 1 cup walnuuts

Instructions

  1. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy, add egss one at a time.
  2. Sift dry ingredients together, mix with wet, then add nuts and chocolate.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 16-23 minutes. (more or less time depending on crispy preference.)
